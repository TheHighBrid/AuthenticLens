# AuthenticLens Apparel Product QA Rules

Use this checklist to interpret the direct audit score for apparel images.

## Product-page readiness checks

- Minimum usable side should ideally be 1200 px or higher.
- Highlight clipping should stay low so white garments, shine, and labels do not blow out.
- Shadow clipping should not erase black fabric, seams, cuffs, or hem detail.
- Texture score should be strong enough to show fabric grain, stitching, ribbing, embroidery, and edges.
- Saturation should stay controlled so colorways look believable and consistent.
- Sharpness should be clean but not crunchy.
- Edge density should not be too low, because low edge structure can mean over-smoothing or weak product detail.
- JPEG blockiness should be low for premium product pages.
- Color cast should be controlled so neutrals and whites remain believable.

## Human visual pass still required

The offline APK checks pixel-level realism. After the app grades the image, manually compare the result against approved references for:

- Collar shape
- Sleeve width and spread
- Jacket or pant length
- Hem alignment
- Zipper placement
- Pocket placement
- Logo and embroidery accuracy
- Product scale across front, back, 45-degree, and macro images
- Background and shadow consistency
