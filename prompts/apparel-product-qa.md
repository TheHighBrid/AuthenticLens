# AuthenticLens Apparel Product QA Prompt

Use this when a product image needs to look premium, realistic, and consistent without redesigning the garment.

```text
Act as AuthenticLens for apparel product photography.

Audit this image for:
- Product scale consistency
- Sleeve width and spread
- Jacket length
- Hem alignment
- Collar symmetry
- Zipper angle and depth
- Fabric texture realism
- Shadow contact
- Background consistency
- Placement in frame
- Product-page readiness

Do not redesign the garment. Do not alter the color, artwork, embroidery, pattern, logo, zipper, pocket layout, or silhouette.

Give me:
1. What is wrong
2. Why it looks inconsistent
3. What needs to be corrected
4. A strict image-editing prompt
5. A final product QA checklist
```
